Chicken

Chicken is the most common type of poultry in the world. Chickens have become prevalent throughout the cuisine of cultures and their meat has been variously adapted to regional tastes.

Beef

Beef is the culinary name for meat from cattle, particularly skeletal muscle. Humans have been eating beef since prehistoric times. Beef is a source of high-quality protein and nutrients.

Sushi

Sushi (すし, 寿司, 鮨, pronounced [sɯꜜɕi] is a Japanese dish of prepared vinegared rice (鮨飯 sushi-meshi), usually with some sugar and salt, accompanying a variety of ingredients.
